Gummi Blocks are the most common blocks used in Gummi ships.
They are obtained by they come in 16 tint variants and 8 shapes.

**Gummi Block**: They are the basic form of gummi blocks, plain blocks offering basic levels of armor.
![gummi block recipe.png](/img/user/gummi%20block%20recipe.png)
They can be tinted as follows:
![gummi block tint recipe.png](/img/user/gummi%20block%20tint%20recipe.png)

**Gummi Shell**: They are medium blocks offering a bit more armor than the basic ones.
![gummi shell recipe.png](/img/user/gummi%20shell%20recipe.png)
Just like Gummi Blocks they can be tinted like this:
![gummi shell tint recipe.png](/img/user/gummi%20shell%20tint%20recipe.png)

**Gummi Dispel**: The strongest variant of gummi blocks, offering more armor.
![gummi dispel recipe.png](/img/user/gummi%20dispel%20recipe.png)
Just like the prevous ones they can be tinted like this
![gummi dispel tint recipe.png](/img/user/gummi%20dispel%20tint%20recipe.png)

**Shapes**:
Any of the three type of Gummi blocks can be placed in a stonecutter to modify their shape.
![gummi block shapes.png](/img/user/gummi%20block%20shapes.png)
Some shapes have special placement properties, once holding in your hand they will change the block placement outline to add a guide.
![gummi edge placement.png](/img/user/gummi%20edge%20placement.png)![gummi corner placement.png](/img/user/gummi%20corner%20placement.png)

**Special Gummi Blocks**:
**Gummi Aero**: They are slim versions of the blocks and they allow and increase the gummi ship to rotate.
![gummi aero1.png](/img/user/gummi%20aero1.png)![gummi aero 2.png](/img/user/gummi%20aero%202.png)

**Gummi Weapons**: They allow the ship to have offensive capabilities.
- Single block weapons:
	- Fire/G:
	They shoot a single, straight orange shot forward.
	![gummi fire.png](/img/user/gummi%20fire.png)
	- Blizzard/G
	They shoot two light blue shots spreading in a V shape.
	![gummi blizzard.png](/img/user/gummi%20blizzard.png)
	- Gravity/G
	They shoot a single, slower purple shot that explodes on impact.
	![gummi gravity.png](/img/user/gummi%20gravity.png)
	- Water/G
	They are activated when the ship starts a boost, they shoot an horizontal blue line that will damage the enemies it touches.
	![gummi water.png](/img/user/gummi%20water.png)


- **Multi block weapons**:
	- Fira/G:
	Their shot is stronger than the one from Fire/G.
	![gummi fira.png](/img/user/gummi%20fira.png)
	- Blizzara/G
	They now shoot three shots forming a triangle.
	![gummi blizzara.png](/img/user/gummi%20blizzara.png)
	- Gravira/G
	Their shot is now stronger and has a bigger explosion area.
	![gummi gravira.png](/img/user/gummi%20gravira.png)
	- Watera/G
	The projectile width will be larger and will deal more damage.
	![gummi watera.png](/img/user/gummi%20watera.png)

**Gummi Engines**: They allow the ship to move.
Vernier/G: Smallest engine.
![gummi vernier.png](/img/user/gummi%20vernier.png)
Thruster/G: Medium engine.
![gummi thruster.png](/img/user/gummi%20thruster.png)


**Gummi Cockpits**: They allow players to sit on the ship, different cockpits have different carrying capacity, they also offer some armor.
Bubble Helm/G: They have a carrying capacity of 3 players.
![gummi bubble helm.png](/img/user/gummi%20bubble%20helm.png)
